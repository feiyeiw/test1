Fully Automatic Paper Cup Production Line | Intelligent Cup Forming, Inspection, Sterilization and Packaging System

This fully automatic paper cup production line integrates cup forming, bottom sealing, inspection, sterilization, counting, stacking, and packaging into a complete smart manufacturing solution. Designed for high-volume paper cup manufacturers and food packaging companies, the system transforms raw PE-coated paper rolls into finished packaged paper cups through a highly automated process requiring minimal manual intervention. With production speeds reaching 80–120 cups per minute, integrated vision inspection systems, UV sterilization modules, intelligent packaging units, and PLC-based automation controls, the line significantly improves manufacturing efficiency, product consistency, and hygiene standards while reducing labor costs. The system is suitable for disposable paper cups, coffee cups, tea cups, beverage cups, takeaway packaging products, and food-service applications.
