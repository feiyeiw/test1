Summary

The rapid growth of electric vehicles, energy storage systems, and industrial electrification has created increasing demand for highly automated battery manufacturing factories.

This project showcases a complete lithium battery production line integrating electrode manufacturing, cell assembly, testing, automation handling, and intelligent warehousing.
