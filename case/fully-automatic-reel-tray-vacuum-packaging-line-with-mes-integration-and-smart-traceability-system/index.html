Fully Automatic Reel & Tray Vacuum Packaging Line | MES Integrated Aluminum Bag Packing System with Labeling & Traceability

This project demonstrates a fully automated Reel & Tray packaging system designed for high-value electronics manufacturing industries such as semiconductors, LED, connectors, and precision electronic components. The system integrates MES-driven traceability, automatic labeling, desiccant insertion, humidity control, aluminum bag vacuum sealing, carton packing, and palletizing. It supports 6–15 inch reels and customized trays, achieving high compatibility and full automation from incoming traceable scanning to final boxed pallet output. The solution significantly improves packaging consistency, reduces labor dependency, and ensures moisture-proof protection for sensitive electronic materials.

Project Overview

Project Overview

This project focuses on developing a fully automated packaging system for Reel and Tray-based electronic components used in semiconductor, LED, and precision electronics industries.

With increasing demand for high-reliability electronic components, packaging is no longer a manual auxiliary process but a critical part of product quality assurance.

The system integrates MES communication, vacuum sealing, moisture protection, labeling automation, and full traceability control into one unified production line.

Key Points

  • Unified Reel & Tray dual-format automation system
  • Full MES-driven traceability and instruction execution
  • Moisture-proof vacuum aluminum bag packaging
  • Automatic desiccant and humidity control integration
  • Flexible compatibility for 6–15 inch Reel sizes
  • Customized Tray handling fixture system
  • End-to-end packaging automation without manual intervention
  • High-speed output with stable industrial performance

Implementation & Workflow

The implementation is designed as a continuous flow automation system.

All operations are synchronized through a central PLC and MES interface.

Key engineering stages include:

Mechanical integration of feeding and transfer modules
Electrical control system synchronization
MES communication protocol configuration
Label printing system calibration
Vacuum sealing pressure control optimization
Conveyor speed balancing and buffering system design

The system ensures stable operation even under high-frequency production environments.

Challenge

In modern electronics manufacturing, packaging is no longer a simple wrapping process but a critical quality control stage. The customer faced several key challenges: High risk of moisture damage in semiconductor and electronic components Inconsistent manual packaging quality Lack of full traceability in Reel and Tray handling Frequent labeling errors and mismatch with MES data Low packaging efficiency and high labor cost Difficulty handling multiple Reel and Tray sizes in one system Risk of vacuum leakage at sealing interfaces Increasing demand for export-level packaging compliance These problems directly affected product yield, customer satisfaction, and global shipment reliability.

Solution

We designed a fully automatic Reel & Tray vacuum packaging line with MES integration. The solution includes: Automatic incoming Reel/Tray barcode scanning MES system data validation and label instruction generation Dual online printing system (product label + bag label) Automatic label application on Reel/Tray and packaging materials Aluminum foil bag automatic feeding and positioning Automatic insertion of desiccant and humidity indicator card Vacuum chamber sealing system with leak-proof control Secondary carton packing automation Carton sealing + external labeling system Automatic palletizing system for finished cartons The system ensures that Reel or Tray is treated as the packaging object itself, regardless of internal electronic components.

Workflow & Layout

Full system workflow: Step 1: Incoming Scanning Reel or Tray is scanned and traceability ID is uploaded to MES. Step 2: MES Instruction Release MES system determines packaging specification and sends label data. Step 3: Automatic Labeling System prints and applies: Reel/Tray label Aluminum bag label Step 4: Product Loading Reel or Tray is placed into aluminum foil bag automatically. Step 5: Moisture Protection Desiccant + humidity indicator card are inserted. Step 6: Vacuum Sealing Aluminum bag is vacuum sealed and thermally closed. Step 7: Carton Packing Sealed bags are automatically transferred into cartons. Step 8: Carton Sealing & Labeling Cartons are sealed and labeled with tracking codes. Step 9: Palletizing Finished cartons are stacked and palletized automatically.
