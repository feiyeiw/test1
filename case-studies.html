Project Overview

[Project Photo/Video]

Client Background

A leading e-commerce company in Southeast Asia needed to upgrade their fulfillment center to handle rapid growth in online orders. The existing manual warehouse could not meet the demand during peak seasons.

Project Goals

  • Increase storage capacity by 200%
  • Improve order fulfillment speed
  • Reduce labor dependency
  • Enable 24/7 operations

Warehouse Layout

[Layout Drawing]

Layout Design

The 45m × 60m warehouse was divided into four zones: receiving, storage, picking, and shipping. The ASRS occupies the central 30m × 40m area with 8-meter high racks.

Key Features

  • 10 aisles with double-deep storage
  • Integrated conveyor system
  • Dedicated returns processing area
  • Future expansion预留空间

System Configuration

[System Diagram]

Equipment List

  • 4 shuttle vehicles per aisle
  • 2 lift systems for level changing
  • Automated conveyor network (200m)
  • WMS with AI optimization
  • 8 pick stations with RF terminals

Software Integration

The system integrates with the client's existing ERP and includes real-time inventory tracking, predictive maintenance alerts, and dynamic slotting optimization.
