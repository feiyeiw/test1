$2.5M Crane ASRS for Pharmaceutical Cold Storage
99.9% Traceability & +150% Efficiency for 15,000 Pallets

A leading pharmaceutical distributor needed strict temperature control, full-batch traceability, and higher throughput. Our Stacker Crane ASRS delivered compliance and performance.

The Challenge

The client operated a temperature-sensitive pharmaceutical warehouse where manual handling created compliance risks and throughput bottlenecks. Key challenges included:

  • Strict regulatory requirements for batch traceability (GDP / GMP)
  • Need for continuous cold-chain temperature monitoring
  • Limited warehouse height requiring vertical storage optimization
  • High labor turnover affecting operational stability

The ASRS Solution

We designed a Stacker Crane ASRS optimized for cold storage environments, with full software integration to support pharmaceutical compliance.

The solution includes:

  • Climate-controlled crane aisles with sealed rack structure
  • Double-deep storage for 15,000 pallet positions
  • WCS/WMS with full batch tracking, FIFO enforcement, and expiry alerts
  • Integration with existing ERP and temperature-monitoring sensors
