Summary

Reel & Tray packaging automation is a critical system in modern electronics manufacturing that enables fully automated handling, labeling, vacuum sealing, moisture protection, and traceability of semiconductor components. This system integrates MES communication, smart labeling, and vacuum aluminum bag packaging to ensure high reliability for sensitive electronic components used in semiconductor, LED, and precision electronics industries.

Technology

  • Reel & Tray dual-format handling system
  • MES-driven production control
  • Automatic barcode scanning & traceability
  • Vacuum aluminum foil packaging
  • Desiccant & humidity card insertion
  • Smart labeling system
  • Industrial conveyor automation
  • PLC-based system control

Challenge

Electronics manufacturers face increasing pressure from global supply chains requiring high reliability, zero defect packaging, and full traceability. Traditional manual packaging systems suffer from labeling errors, inconsistent sealing quality, lack of MES integration, and high labor costs. Additionally, moisture-sensitive components are easily damaged during storage and transport.

Solution

We provide a fully automated Reel & Tray packaging system that integrates MES control, automatic labeling, vacuum sealing, and moisture protection. The system treats Reel or Tray as the packaging object and ensures standardized handling regardless of internal electronic components.

Workflow & Layout

Incoming Reel/Tray scanning
MES data verification
Label printing and application
Automatic aluminum bag loading
Desiccant insertion
Vacuum sealing
Carton packaging
Palletizing

Results & ROI

  • Labor reduction: 70–90%
  • Packaging speed: up to 200 units/hour
  • Defect rate: near zero
  • ROI: 12–24 months
  • Improved traceability compliance

Project Overview / Opening

This system is designed for electronics manufacturers requiring high reliability packaging for Reel and Tray components. It integrates automation, MES communication, and vacuum sealing technologies into a unified production line.

Key Points

  • Fully automated workflow

Implementation / Workflow

System implementation includes mechanical integration, MES configuration, labeling calibration, and vacuum sealing optimization to ensure stable production under high throughput conditions.
