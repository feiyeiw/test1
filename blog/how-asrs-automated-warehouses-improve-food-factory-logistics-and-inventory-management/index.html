Summary

This article explains how ASRS (Automated Storage and Retrieval Systems) improve logistics efficiency, inventory accuracy, and supply chain performance in modern food factories, especially tomato processing and food packaging plants.

Technology

  • Automated Storage and Retrieval System (ASRS)
  • Warehouse control system (WCS)
  • Warehouse management system (WMS)
  • PLC automation control system
  • Conveyor logistics system
  • Robotic stacker cranes
  • Barcode / RFID tracking system
  • IoT inventory monitoring system
  • Smart logistics optimization system
  • Integrated food factory MES system
  • AI-based warehouse scheduling system

Challenge

7.Traditional food factory warehouses face major inefficiencies:

7.1 Manual handling causes high labor dependency
7.2 Inventory errors due to human tracking systems
7.3 Low storage density and wasted warehouse space
7.4 Slow inbound and outbound logistics operations
7.5 Difficulty managing large SKU food products
7.6 High operational cost in storage and distribution
7.7 Lack of real-time inventory visibility

Solution

8.ASRS automated warehouse systems solve these problems by:

8.1 Fully automated storage and retrieval process
8.2 High-density vertical storage utilization
8.3 Real-time inventory tracking via WMS system
8.4 Automated inbound and outbound logistics flow
8.5 Integration with production lines and packaging systems
8.6 Reduced human intervention in warehouse operations
8.7 AI-based warehouse optimization scheduling
8.8 Seamless connection with ASRS + food production systems

Workflow & Layout

9.1 Finished food products arrive from packaging line
9.2 Barcode/RFID scanning system identifies inventory
9.3 Conveyor system transfers goods into ASRS input station
9.4 Stacker crane automatically stores products in racks
9.5 WMS system records real-time inventory data
9.6 Retrieval request generated by logistics system
9.7 ASRS retrieves required pallets automatically
9.8 Output conveyor sends goods to shipping area

Results & ROI

  • 10.1 Warehouse labor reduced by 50–90%
  • 10.2 Storage efficiency significantly improved
  • 10.3 Inventory accuracy reaches near 99.9%
  • 10.4 Faster inbound and outbound logistics speed
  • 10.5 Reduced warehouse operating cost
  • 10.6 Improved space utilization (2–5x density increase)
  • 10.7 Better supply chain visibility
  • 10.8 Higher ROI in factory logistics automation
