$1.2M Shuttle ASRS for E-commerce Warehouse
Increase Efficiency by 180% with 10,000 Pallet Capacity

A Southeast Asian e-commerce fulfillment center transformed its warehouse operations with a tailored Shuttle ASRS — reducing labor by 60% and achieving ROI in just 2.5 years.

Industry: E-commerce Fulfillment Location: Southeast Asia System: Shuttle ASRS Capacity: 10,000 Pallets Throughput: 200 pallets/hour Investment: $1.2 Million ROI: ~2.5 Years

The Challenge

The client was facing rapid growth in online orders, but their traditional warehouse setup could not keep up. Key challenges included:

  • Increasing labor costs and difficulty hiring workers
  • Low picking efficiency during peak seasons
  • Limited warehouse space and poor vertical utilization
  • High error rates in manual operations

The ASRS Solution

To address these challenges, we designed and implemented a Shuttle-based ASRS system tailored for high-density e-commerce operations.

The solution includes:

  • Multi-level shuttle racking system (up to 8.6 meters high)
  • Automated shuttle carriers for pallet movement
  • Conveyor system for inbound and outbound handling
  • Integrated WMS system connected with the client’s ERP
