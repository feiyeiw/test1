$800K Mini Load ASRS for Electronics / 3C Fulfillment
+200% Efficiency in 50% Less Floor Space

A fast-growing 3C electronics brand needed to handle thousands of small SKUs in a tight urban warehouse. Our Mini Load ASRS delivered speed, accuracy, and space savings without expanding the building.

Industry: Electronics / 3C Location: East Asia System: Mini Load ASRS Capacity: 5,000 Totes Throughput: 300 totes/hour Investment: $800K ROI: ~2 Years

The Challenge

The client sold consumer electronics and accessories online, with thousands of small, lightweight SKUs and fast-changing demand. Their existing shelving-based warehouse was reaching its limits. Key challenges included:

  • Limited floor space in an expensive urban logistics park
  • Thousands of small SKUs with frequent turnover
  • High order-error rates from manual picking
  • Need to scale for promotional peaks (e.g., 11.11, Black Friday)

The ASRS Solution

We designed a compact Mini Load ASRS using tote-based storage and high-speed extractors — ideal for small parts and dense urban warehouses.

The solution includes:

  • Tote-based mini load racking (up to 6 meters high)
  • High-speed extractor for rapid tote retrieval
  • Pick-to-light stations for accurate order fulfillment
  • WMS with real-time inventory and wave-picking optimization
