$4M Multi-Shuttle ASRS for Automotive Parts Warehouse
450 Picks/Hour & +220% Efficiency Across 35,000 Pallets

A global automotive supplier needed massive throughput, multi-shift reliability, and dense storage for thousands of SKUs. Our Multi-Shuttle ASRS became the backbone of their distribution network.

Industry: Automotive Parts Location: North America System: Multi-Shuttle ASRS Capacity: 35,000 Pallets Throughput: 450 pallets/hour Investment: $4 Million ROI: ~4 Years

The Challenge

The client supplied components to multiple OEM plants and needed to maintain strict delivery schedules while handling thousands of SKUs. Key challenges included:

  • Extremely high throughput requirements across multiple shifts
  • Need for FIFO/LIFO flexibility depending on part type
  • Large physical footprint with poor space utilization
  • Seasonal demand spikes causing fulfillment delays

The ASRS Solution

We implemented a large-scale Multi-Shuttle ASRS with redundant aisles and high-speed conveyors to support just-in-time automotive logistics.

The solution includes:

  • 12 aisles of multi-shuttle racking (up to 12 meters high)
  • Dual shuttle carriers per level for redundancy and speed
  • High-speed conveyor loop with pick stations and quality gates
  • WMS integrated with client MRP and sequencing systems
